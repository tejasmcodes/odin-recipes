Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Learn how to make grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classsic hot sandwich.

  • Prep Time : 5 minss
  • cook Time : 10 mins
  • Total Time : 15 mins
  • servings : 2

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grille cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

How to Make The Best Grilled Cheese

Here's a very brief overview of what you should do :

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on the hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, thenplace another slice of bread on top(butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. continue cooking until the cheese is melted

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoon butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese
