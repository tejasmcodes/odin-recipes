Oreo Milkshake

This Oreo Milksahke is easy to make, super kid-friendly, and delicious - a favorite on a hot day!

  • Prep Time : 10 mins
  • Total Time : 10 mins
  • Servings : 2

How Do You Make a Delicious Oreo Milkshake ?

Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together ina blender until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses.

Ingredients

  • 6 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®)
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • ¾ cup milk

Nutrition Facts

  • 249 Calories
  • 11g Fat
  • 33g Carbs
  • 6g Protein
